Piano Lessons Tailored for You

All ages are welcome to take piano lessons. Brian offers a traditional approach as he works to develop a solid technique and a flluid ability play classical, jazz, and modern styles. It's not just a piaono lesson, it's a complete in-depth and fun music program. The lessons include music theory, ear training and sight reading which are tailored to each students skill and needs.

RCM Certified Teacher

Brian is a RCM certified teacher and qualifies under the category of an Advanced Piano Specialist. He is also a member of the Ontario Registered Music Teachers Association

Home or Studio Lessons

Brian organizes two locations for studio piano lessons. One is in Richmond St. and the other on Dundas.
Home lessons are available in the London area

Tuition for Piano Lessons

Brian conducts weekly piano lessons. The length depends on age, level, and the time for practice. The fee for lessons is $60 per hour. Students are required to pay for four lessons in advance. The cost of four 30-minute lessons is $120.
Lessons policies followed are those recommended by the Ontario Registered Music Teachers Association

  • Music conveys our cultural heritage. It is as important to know about Beethoven and Louis Armstrong as it is know about Einstein and Newton

  • Music teaches studenst about their relationship with others, in both their own and foreign cultures

  • Music enhances scholastic performance

  • Music offers students avenues of success they may not experience elsewhere in their education curriculum

  • Music helps students learn that not all life is quantifiable

  • Music exhalts the human spirit
